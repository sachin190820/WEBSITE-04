How much time am I'm actually getting for my money?

We take a value-based approach. We ensure that our work is of the highest standards by a) moving quickly but not rushing, b) working with a team of incredibly senior editors, c) following an established process with checks and balances.

How do I communicate feedback/revisions?

Simple changes are typically captured in writing. If bigger changes are needed, you can request time with a Project Lead or send feedback through Slack/Trello.
We also encourage you to interact with the Frame.io videos we share with you. We make one for each request we complete.

When does my month start? Can I pause?

We work in 30 day blocks of time. Your 30 days begins when we start working on your first video, not right when you pay. You can pause your plan at any time.

How do I know you won 't delay the turnaround time?

Why would we? The more great work we can do for our clients, the better! Every piece of delivered content is an opportunity to expand our brand.

I’m unhappy with the work. How do I get a refund?

While you’re welcome to cancel your subscription, we do not offer refunds. Request a cancelation in writing at least 48 hours before your subscription renews.

How will you share your work?

We create a new project for each client. A kanban board is always accessible to you as an active contributor. We update the board accordingly when work is delivered to you upon completion. When a request is completed, we will create a Frame.io video for your review.

Why wouldn’t I just hire a Freelancer?

You could, but quality, process, and communication are always a risk. In our experience, it often doesn’t end well unless you have ample experience managing freelancers, which is a headache in itself.We remove all the risk by managing the entire process and quality checks for you.

What do I need to give you to get started?

Once you pay for your subscription you’ll receive onboarding information from your dedicated account manager. We will then work with you to articulate your requests for our team to start working on.

Is there a limit on file size?

Nope!
